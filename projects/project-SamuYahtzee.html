Samu Yahtzee

A rogue-like game based on the mechanic of Yahtzee.

YouTube itch.io Pitch Document Rules of the Game

Overview

Samu Yahtzee is a solo project developed during my time at the academy. Starting from the classic dice-rolling mechanic of Yahtzee, I built an original rogue-like game system around it — with combat, exploration, and player progression — first as a physical tabletop game, then ported into a digital version.

Core Systems

  • Dice-based combat with risk/reward decision-making at every turn
  • Drop mechanic — when an enemy is defeated the player obtain a drop that give bonus on top of basic Yahtzee dice calculations
  • Fight mechanic — turn-based encounters with custom resolution rules
  • Complete ruleset designed from scratch, balancing player agency with randomness

Design Notes

The challenge was preserving the tension of Yahtzee's push-your-luck decisions while adding meaningful strategic layers. The project shipped with a full pitch document and rules of the game document, both available to download above.
