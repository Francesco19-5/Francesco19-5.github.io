The Strongest Strongman

A GMTK Game Jam 2024 entry — punch iron cubes that squish and stretch when blocked by walls and floors.

itch.io

Overview

The Strongest Strongman is a GMTK Game Jam 2024 entry made by team Sailing Rocks. You play as a strongman whose strength literally reshapes the world: hitting an iron cube sends it flying, but if a wall or the floor blocks its path, the cube deforms — squishing flat or stretching tall depending on the collision.

Core Mechanics

  • Iron cube deformation — cubes that are blocked by a surface squish or stretch rather than stopping
  • Physics-driven puzzle interactions built around the deformation system
  • Exaggerated strongman feel with impactful hit feedback

Technical Notes

Built in Unity with C# during the jam sprint. The deformation system was the core technical focus: detecting the blocking surface and applying the correct scale transform to communicate the mechanic clearly and satisfyingly to the player.
